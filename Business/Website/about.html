About Snowcrest

(the story of us)

Snowcrest Mountain Sports is a small film production group based in Helena, Montana. The team of four work hard to produce the best skiing and mountain biking videos, along with some vlogs and all of the store merchandise. Snowcrest was created in 2015 by Alex Thompson and a few freinds. The group made a couple videos and then forgot about the whole thing. Now a year has passed and they're back at it again, playing the the great outdoors.
